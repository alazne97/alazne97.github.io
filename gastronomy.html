Amazing food

Our gastronomy it's good quality. We have a three Michelin star restaurant in San Sebastian called "Arzak". This restaurant adapts the traditional gastronomy of the Basque Country to the new times making it one of the most innovative of the world.

Bacalao al pil pil

Bacalao means cod and pil pil is the sauce that throws over the fish. This recipe is very easy, you only cod, olive oil, salt, chili rings and garlic cloves. You should try this food in The Basque Country because our fish is very good, we are in the coast.

Txipirones

Txipirones are inky squid. It is a common food for Christmas. It may seem disgusting because of its black ink, but I assure you that are delicious. To prepare this dish you need the squids, vegetables, ink, white wine and garlic.

Marmitako

Marmitako is a classic Basque meal and it means "from the pot". To prepare this dish you need tuna, onion, potatoes, garlic, tomato, parsley, white wine, olive oil, water, salt and dried pepper. Instead of slicing the potato you should break it so it can preserve its natural structure.
