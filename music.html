Great music

Nowadays there are many groups that sing in vasc, but one of the most succesful groups in The Basque Country is Berri Txarrak, that means "Bad News". It was founded in 1994 in Navarre and they play alternative rock. Despite singing in vasc they make international routes and people all over the worl love their music. They have been playing in the same stage with Rise Against and they have a song together. One of the most interesting facts about this group is that in june of 2017 they performed for one person in Paris because he was the only one that was there. However, they gave everything they have...This is what it means to have respect for the public.

Txalaparta

This percussion instrument consist in a table of long wooden boards and thick sticks. It can be played by one or more performers. the sound made by the sticks when touching the wood can be very relaxing.

Trikitixa

The first written evidence of this two-row bottom accordion was in 1889. It was probably introduced by the French or Italian immigrants coming from the Alps. One of the most famous players of "trikitixa" is Kepa Junkera, who mixes traditional and modern sounds with this instrument.

Alboka

This is a single-reed woodwind instrument traditionally made from animal horn. This instruments is native to The Basque Country but you can see similar instruments in other autonomous communities of Spain.

Txistu

This instruments, that was first mentionated in 1864, is a kind of fipple flute with three holes that makes a sound of a whistle. Players usually accompain the "txistu" with a tabor.
