Beautiful landscapes

Biscay

Biscay is located on the weast f the Basque Country and its capital is Bilbao. This autonomous community has very lovely beaches.

Guipuzcoa

Gipuzcoa is located in the east of the Basque Country and its capital is San Sebastian.

Alava

Alava is located in the south of Basque Country and its capital is Victoria. This autonomous community is well-known for its potatoes.

French Basque Country

French Basque Country, also known as "Iparralde", used to be part of The Basque Country. In the picture, you can see the beach of Biarritz that is located in Bayonne.
