Aurresku

Aurresku refers to a typical dance of Euskadi and consist on a reverence. Is very popular at weddings and in public acts.

Harrijasotzaile

This rural sport consist on raising a big stone from the ground putting it before in the shoulder. The stones have different shape and size and they can reach the 212,5 kilos.

Football

The most famous football clubs are "Athletic club" from Biscay and "Real Sociedad" from Guipuzcoa. Athletic club, founded in 1898, has played all the editions of the First Division of Spain since its creation. Is also well-known because all members of the team are from the Basque Country.
