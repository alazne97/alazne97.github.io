Basque language

Basque language, or "euskera" is a very ancient language and is unrelated to other languages of Europe. Its origin is not certain but the basque alphabet uses the latin script. Most of the linguists say that is the oldest living language of Europe. The word to say "hello" in vasc is "kaixo".

Basque americans

According to the census of United States, there are 57,793 Americans of full or partial Basque descendent, and the largest state of Basque-American population is California. This is because it was a Basque immigration after the Spanish Carlist Wars in the 1830s.
