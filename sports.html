Original sports

Harrijasotzaile

This rural sport consist on raising a big stone from the ground putting it before in the shoulder. The stones have different shape and size and they can reach the 212,5 kilos.

Aizkolaritza

This sport consist in a wood-chopping competition and is commonly held at town festivals. The one who finishes first cutting the trunk wins.

Sokatira

Tug of war is international and has many origins because its simplicity. But in The Basque Country is a rural sport that used to be a figth between opposite poles.

Football

The most famous football clubs are "Athletic club" from Biscay and "Real Sociedad" from Guipuzcoa. Athletic Club, founded in 1898, has played all the editions of the First Division of Spain since its creation. It is also well-known because all members of the team are from the Basque Country.
